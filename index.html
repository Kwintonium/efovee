About

Efovee provides custom geometric camera calibration solutions and analytics for your camera's needs.

Geometric calibration is becoming increasingly more important in such industries as automotive, aerospace, and many others. Machine vision cameras are improving rapidly in quality every year, but the demand for accuracy is increasing at the same rate. At Efovee we strive to understand your camera's accuracy needs, and we provide meaningful statistics on what accuracies can be achieved (more than just reprojection error).

 

Efovee currently provides the following services to its customers:

 

Camera Calibration

Efovee can calibrate your cameras for you. We provide custom calibration to get the highest accuracy camera model possible.

Benchmarking

Efovee can test your cameras to determine what accuracies can be achieved, to perform competitor analysis, or assess feasibility.

Validation and Analytics

Efovee can validate your cameras at range providing you with a meaningful real world understanding of your camera's accuracy.

 

Efovee is currently working on the following features...

Online API

If you already have your own calibration setup, you can call the Efovee API to calibrate your cameras.

Test Fixture

Efovee is developing a calibration machine that will automate the calibration process. Current calibration runtime is around 9-12 seconds.

Calibration

Efovee provides custom geometric calibration solutions to get the best accuracy model derived from your cameras. We are able to meet very tight accuracy requirements achieving mean reprojection error orders of magnitude lower than open source alternatives and competitors.

We achieve such accuracies by customizing unique solutions for every camera. Our calibration process and software are very flexible, and certainly will be customized to maximize each camera model's accuracy. Calibration statistics are also provided which will help you understand in which regions your camera meaningfully performs.

Validation

Reprojection error is certainly a meaningful statistic ubiquitous to geometric camera calibration, but reprojection error is influenced by the bundle adjustment while estimating the parameters.

So how does the camera perform in reality?

At Efovee, we validate cameras at range in order to provide you with meaningful statistics about how your camera is performing out in the field. This test is done as means of validating a camera calibration methodology ensuring that overall calibration accuracy is statistically significant.

*Lens: Arducam M40180H10 (100deg) | Sensor: Sony IMX323

Of course, we use reprojection error as well!

 

OpenCV Comparison

It can be seen that Efovee's method is 100 times more accurate than open source alternatives - such as OpenCV - which is accurate on the order of meters, not millimeters. 20% error is equal to 1.4m measured to model distance error.
